Our Values

Our ensemble members embrace all sexualities, political parties, religions, and different forms of identifications. No i.d. is required to enjoy any of our shows! We are of course especially attuned to welcoming people from countries of origins that aren’t that of the already-great U.S. of A. We therefore strive to volunteer our time with local organizations that pay particular focus on immigration issues such as The Young Center for Immigrant Children’s Rights.
